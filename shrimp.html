Firecracker Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 (8 pound) fully cooked spiral-cut ham
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 3 tablespoons whole grain mustard
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons honey

Directions

Step 1

Whisk together 1 cup honey, butter, and Dijon in a small bowl. Brush ham thoroughly with honey mustard butter mixture making sure to get inside all the layers.

Step 2

Place ham face down in the slow cooker. Cover and cook on Low for 7 to 8 hours. Remove 1/2 cup ham drippings and set aside. Continue to cook ham on Low while preparing the glaze.

Step 3

Meanwhile, for glaze, in a saucepan over medium-high heat, add pineapple juice, orange juice, whole grain mustard, brown sugar, 3 tablespoons honey, and reserved ham drippings. Bring to a simmer and cook until glaze starts to thicken and is reduced by half.

Step 4

Apply the glaze to ham, cover, and cook on Low for about 1 more hour.

Step 5

Carefully transfer ham to a serving platter and brush on extra glaze before serving.
